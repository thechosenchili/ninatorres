Transaction Analysis Model

Made during I was a project management resource of a P2P department transition. This model calculates the number of FTEs needed for each role based on the time and motion study conducted for a purchasing department. Input includes process steps for purchasing activities, frequency, and volume, taking into account specific assumptions enumerated in the assumptions tab.
